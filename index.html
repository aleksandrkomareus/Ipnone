All models are equipped with A16 Bionic single-chip system, 48-megapixel main camera and ProMotion screens with Ceramic Shield protection and frequency range of 1 - 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The iPhone 15 Pro has been released in a new design - without the trademark "bangs". There is also a new color in the range. The smartphone, unlike the "regular" 15-iPhones, has an A16 Bionic processor, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
